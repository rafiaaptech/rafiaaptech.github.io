Expert Tips for Plant Care

Choose the Right Plants

Select plants that are suitable for your climate,
available light, and space. Consider factors like indoor vs. outdoor,
sunlight availability, and your own time commitment.

Monitor Humidity:

Some plants require higher humidity levels.
You can mist the leaves or use a humidity tray to increase moisture
around the plant. Conversely, ensure good air circulation to prevent mold
and fungal growth.

Pruning and Deadheading:

Regularly remove dead or yellowing leaves, spent flowers,and
any diseased parts. Pruning promotes healthy growth and prevents pests and diseases.
