Houseleek

About

Sempervivum is a genus of about 40 species of flowering plants in the family Crassulaceae, commonly known as houseleeks. Other common names include liveforever and hen and chicks, a name shared with plants of other genera as well. They are succulent perennials forming mats composed of tufted leaves in rosettes.

Common name:

Sempervivum tectorum

Scientific name:

Sempervivum

Origin Country:

Europe, Morocco, and western Asia.

Watering Cycle:

How often to water Houseleek? once every 1-2 weeks.
